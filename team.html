TEAM

CATHIE REID

Managing Director

Cathie has long been an early tech adopter, as exhibited by the fact that her calendar and contacts carry an unbroken 18 years of history from their origins in her Palm Pilot! Since those heady days she has journeyed enthusiastically through the worlds of Nokia, Blackberry, Apple and most recently Google as one of the few non US based participants in the Google Glass Explorer program. She has tested just about every style of wearable device and can’t wait for the day when biometric identification will remove the need for passwords and link all of her technology seamlessly.

The common theme through each of these technological adventures has been her ability to identify new and innovative utilisations in healthcare, her primary field of interest. Epic Digital is the culmination of those travels, building products that connect healthcare professionals with each other and their patients in a manner that excites, engages and most importantly, improves outcomes.

In addition to her role as MD at Epic Digital Cathie is Managing Partner of the Epic Pharmacy Group, and blogs about all things Epic at cathiereid.com.

EMMA CROSTON

General Manager

Emma loves a challenge and she has found working with businesses to transform them from traditional operations into multi-channel digital business. She loves working with new technologies to find solutions for today’s environment.

She’s an advocate for responsive design and avoids developing native products where possible. She believes social media strategies have their place, but they need to be aligned with the overall business strategy. Having started her career in marketing, Emma also has a passion for consumer psychology and growing strong consumer brands and she’s recently embarked on a start-up of her own.

She’s launched a designer dog label with fashionable, adjustable dog harnesses and collars. Winston Manner is the label, aptly named after Winston the pug who provides her with daily inspiration and is the face of the brand.

TRUDY WORDEN

Head of Product

Trudy has worked across TV, interactive TV, mobile and web in a range of industries and on a number of award winning digital projects. She crossed over from ‘traditional’ media to digital media after working in the film and TV industries and now considers ‘digital’ media ‘traditional.’

Trudy has a serious dislike for Times New Roman font but is passionate about creating great digital user experiences. She specialises in Digital Product Management, UX/UI and team management. Trudy is excited to be working on digital products that enable customers to manage their own health information and outcomes, and improving healthcare staff workflow.

In her spare time Trudy loves to travel (35+ countries; favourite locations include Borneo, Peru and New York), is a part time marriage celebrant (loves a good proposal story), writes a blog and participates in charity bike rides. Trudy also loves to watch shows about, and sometimes participates in, home renovation (currently updating her laundry). In her household, a handsome and cheeky boxer named Dexter rules the roost.
